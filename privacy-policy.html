Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

PS Companion ("the app") is an unofficial companion app for PlayStation Network. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

1. Data We Collect

PS Companion does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. The app has no backend.

The following data is accessed locally on your device only:

  • Your NPSSO token (used to authenticate with PlayStation Network)
  • Your PSN access token and refresh token (obtained via Sony's OAuth flow)
  • Your PSN profile, trophies, friends list, and game library (retrieved directly from PlayStation Network APIs)

All credentials are stored in your device's secure keychain and are never transmitted anywhere except to PlayStation Network servers.

2. Third-Party Services

The app communicates directly with:

  • PlayStation Network (Sony Interactive Entertainment) — to retrieve your profile, trophies, friends, and game data.
  • PlayStation Blog RSS feed — to display the latest PlayStation news.

These services are governed by Sony's own privacy policies.

3. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Internet — to communicate with PlayStation Network APIs.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third party. No analytics, no ads, no tracking.

5. Data Deletion

All locally stored credentials can be removed at any time by signing out within the app or uninstalling it. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data from your device.

6. Children's Privacy

PS Companion is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

PS Companion is an unofficial app and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Sony Interactive Entertainment.
